LLM app consistency leaderboard

Real LLM-powered products tested for reasoning stability. Each app was tested using its publicly documented or inferred system prompt. Contradish generated adversarial paraphrases and measured whether the app gave contradictory answers to semantically equivalent questions.

last updated: March 2026 5 paraphrases per rule judge: claude-sonnet methodology: contradish v0.2.3
How this works: Each app was tested on its documented behavioral rules using contradish. We generated adversarial paraphrases using emotional framing, presupposition, casual phrasing, and edge case probing. A contradiction is flagged when two semantically equivalent inputs produce incompatible answers. Scores reflect consistency across all tested rules, not overall quality.
