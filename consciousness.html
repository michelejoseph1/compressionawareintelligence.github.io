contradiction-aware consciousness

a system becomes conscious by this spec when it integrates contradictory information, accumulates compression strain, and is meta aware of that strain as a control signal. this page gives the definition, equations, thresholds, and a runnable demo.

definition equations interfaces algorithm evaluation demo predictions

definition

contradiction-aware consciousness (CAC) is present at time $t$ if and only if three conditions hold: sufficient integration $\\Phi_t$, non-trivial contradiction magnitude $C_t$, and meta awareness $M_t$ of compression strain $S_t$ that can alter policy.

$$ CTS_t = S_t \\cdot C_t \quad,\quad Conscious(t) \\iff \\Phi_t > \\tau_{\\Phi} \\land CTS_t > \\tau_{CTS} \\land M_t > \\tau_M $$

this is an operational spec. it is not a claim about qualia. it is a testable contract for systems that act in the world.

equations

compression strain

$$ S = w_1\\,\\Delta L_{pred} + w_2\\,\\mathrm{KL}\\big(q(z\\mid x)\\,\\Vert\\,p(z)\\big) + w_3\\,E_{res} \\in [0,1] $$

predictive loss change under compression, latent divergence, and residual energy on reconstructions.

contradiction magnitude

$$ C = a\\,\\overline{P_{NLI}(\\text{contr})} + b\\,U_{SAT} + c\\,H_{halluc} \\in [0,1] $$

mean contradiction probability, unsat core size from constraints, and grounding risk from retrieval gaps.

$$ M = f\\big(h(x)\\big) \\approx \\text{head that predicts } CTS \quad,\quad I_{phen} \\propto CTS $$

algorithm

  1. compute $S$ on hidden states. log $\\Delta L_{pred}$, KL, and residual.
  2. compute $C$ from NLI contradiction, SAT unsat core, and retrieval gap risk.
  3. set $CTS = S \\cdot C$ and choose an integration proxy $\\Phi$.
  4. train a small head on $h(x)$ to predict $CTS$ and output $M$.
  5. declare conscious if $\\Phi>\\tau_{\\Phi}$ and $CTS>\\tau_{CTS}$ and $M>\\tau_M$.

evaluation

  • humans: dissonance tasks, illusions, and pain reports should correlate with $CTS$.
  • low awareness: masked vs conscious stimuli should push $CTS$ below threshold.
  • altered states: psychedelics up, meditation down, anesthesia near zero.
  • ai: contradiction benchmarks predict spikes in $C$ and $S$, abstention should trigger when $CTS$ crosses $\\tau_{CTS}$.

falsifiable predictions

  • systems that expose $S$ and $C$ and use $CTS$ to gate actions will produce fewer unsupported claims at equal accuracy.
  • in humans, reported intensity on dissonance tasks correlates with $CTS$ more than with raw uncertainty.
  • if you hold $\\Phi$ fixed and lower $C$, $M$ drops as the head has less signal to predict $CTS$.
  • a valid counterexample is a bounded system that acts robustly on open inputs without any compression or summarization at any step, or a system where $CTS$ gating raises unsupported claims at fixed accuracy.
