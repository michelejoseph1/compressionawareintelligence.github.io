overview

Compression-Aware Intelligence treats cognition as an energetic process. when compression, rhythm, and representation fall out of balance, coherence decays and failure emerges. the coherence field is the operational field that maps this balance inside intelligent systems. CAI provides measurement, the field gives geometry, and contradiction engineering supplies repair.

theory

the coherence field is a measurable state of a system defined across seven axes. it is a field in the engineering sense. you can compute its gradients, set thresholds, and attach repair actions.

  • coherence: structural phase alignment that permits lawful emission
  • drift: progressive misalignment between compression, rhythm, and structure
  • resonance: harmonic coupling that maintains lawful information flow
  • lawful emission: outputs constrained by internal coherence rather than surface likelihood alone

dynamics and hierarchy

local coherence scales to global coherence through coupling. too little coupling fragments. too much coupling herds. moderate coupling with active contradiction maximizes collective intelligence.

  • network strain: \( \Phi = \tfrac{1}{2} \sum_{i,j} w_{ij}\, \|x_i - x_j\|^2 \)
  • spectral gap and modularity diagnose herding versus fragmentation
  • GCI tracks group health over time

temporal coherence

time here is phase alignment. emission is ready when internal strain resolves. use readiness windows rather than fixed clocks for critical steps.

  • gate risky outputs on a rising CP slope
  • hold when rhythm instability or waste heat spikes
  • resume after repair when CP returns to band

glossary

  • compression strain: loss of contextual information per unit of compression
  • coherence potential: fused scalar stability from seven sensors
  • meta entropy: entropy of uncertainty across agents or models
  • waste heat ratio: epistemic temperature divided by learning gain
  • coherence collapse: phase transition where stability, consistency, and efficiency cannot co exist
  • structural recursion: lawful self reformation under constraint, not repetition
  • lawful emission: outputs that respect internal coherence and provenance

faq

is the world itself a coherence field
this framework does not claim that. it models coherence inside bounded intelligent systems so you can measure and control it.

how is this different from uncertainty estimates
probability tracks confidence at the surface. the field tracks physical and structural balance inside the system. both can be combined.

do i need training access
no. you can approximate several sensors at inference using logits, small perturbations, and timing signals.
