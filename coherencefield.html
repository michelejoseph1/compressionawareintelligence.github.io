theory

The Coherence Field is a measurable state of a system defined across seven axes. It is not a metaphysical claim about the world. It is a model that permits measurement and control inside bounded intelligent systems.

  • coherence: structural phase alignment that permits lawful emission
  • drift: progressive misalignment between compression, rhythm, and structure
  • resonance: harmonic coupling that maintains lawful information flow

lawful emission means outputs are constrained by internal coherence, not surface likelihood alone.
