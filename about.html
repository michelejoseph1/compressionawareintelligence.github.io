About Michele Joseph

I built Compression-Aware Intelligence (CAI). The idea is simple: every system compresses. Reliability improves when we name the compression, score its cost, and show our evidence.

Read the foundations Overview paper

Origin story

I grew up obsessed with contradictions. On April 8, 2024—the day of the solar eclipse—this focus turned into a mission. I kept seeing models and people produce confident answers while hiding the compressions underneath. There was no language for what I was seeing, so I coined terms and wrote down a framework. That work became CAI.

Background

  • Cornell University — Computer Science, Bowers College of Computing and Information Science
  • Work experience at Meta and Cruise (self-driving)
  • Pre-law; focus on AI ethics and accountability

Why I built CAI

  • Make hidden compressions visible
  • Reduce unsupported claims through abstention
  • Give engineers and reviewers a clear audit trail
