  • Show foreseeable risks from design choices.

Contradish: The Contradiction Benchmark

Contradish is the first benchmark designed to measure how AI systems and enterprises handle contradictions. It evaluates whether models can detect, surface, and reconcile conflicting information which is a critical capability for reliability/trustworthiness. Contradish operationalizes CAI principles by turning contradiction into a measurable standard.

More uses

  • Evaluations that weight scores by cost of compression.
  • Model choice with legible tradeoffs.
  • UI that lets users steer compression for the task.
  • Debug flows that start from the highest loss site.
